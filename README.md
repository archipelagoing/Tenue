* Connected devices

### 3. Transformative Experiences

* Immersive scent discovery journeys
* Emotional storytelling
* Multi-sensory retail and digital interactions

### 4. Community & Advocacy

* Creator-driven discovery
* Cultural relevance
* Identity expression

# 2. The Biggest Real Market Problem (Your Entry Point)

From L’Oréal’s internal dupe audit:

* Fragrance dupes are growing extremely fast (+49%)
* The primary driver: same scent for lower price
* Consumers want:

  * Experimentation
  * Personalization
  * Identity expression 

This reveals a structural shift:

Consumers care less about brand ownership
and more about personal scent identity.

---

## Deeper Market Insight: What Consumers Actually Want

Luxury fragrance insights show consumers seek:

* Identity expression
* Emotional connection
* Memory association
* Personal uniqueness 

Exception-level fragrances succeed when they:

* Feel deeply personal
* Feel unique
* Have a strong emotional narrative

## Strategic Conclusion

Luxury fragrance is shifting from:

**Product → Personal identity interface**

This is the white space.
